What is Ripple?

  • A company that created the Ripple payment protocol.

  • A payment protocol for handling international transactions with a focus on speed and transparency.

  • A token (cryptocurrency) that is intended to be used as a form of fiat in the Ripple payment protocol

What is XRP?

It is an abbreviation for the cryptocurrency developed by Ripple the company.

How do I acquire XRP?

XRP is only available through digital currency exchanges online.

How do I store XRP?

XRP are stored in digital wallets

What is a Digital Wallet?

A digital wallet is a base58 encoded hash. It is a public address that can be used to access public account information as well as sending and receiving XRP.

How do I send XRP to a Digital Wallet?

Sending XRP to a wallet is called a Transaction. A Transaction must be created through the RippleAPI, or one of the digital currency exchanges online. Once a transaction is created, it must be authenticated with the secret key of the wallet being withdrawn from. Finally, it must be submitted to the Ripple network for the transaction to be recorded into the public ledger, and the transfer of XRP to be actualized.

How do I create a Transaction?

To create a transaction requires at least two public addresses: The wallet being withdrawn from, and the wallet being deposited to.
